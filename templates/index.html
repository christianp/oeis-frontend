The main uses for the OEIS are to identify a sequence, and to obtain more terms, formulas, references, links, programs, etc. for a sequence.

You can search for integer sequences by entering the first few terms, or some words to describe it.

Leave off the first term or two, because people may disagree about where the sequence begins.

Examples of searches

  • 1,4,9,16,15,36,64 - sequences containing this subsequence.
  • 5 8 13 233 39088169 - sequences containing these terms.
  • "Fermat's little theorem" - entries with this text in any field.
  • author:guy keyword:nice
